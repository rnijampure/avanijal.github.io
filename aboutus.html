About the company

Avanijal Agri Automation Pvt. Ltd. was born to address the farmer's irrigation needs. Avanijal designs and develops Irrigation Systems/Controllers to helps farmers to grow more with less water, labour and electricity. Easy to irrigate, fertigate and flush water filter through mobile phones and avoids odd hour farm visits. The controller supports basic time, volume and sensor based precision irrigation. It also supports wired and wireless valve/sensor connections. Supports irrigation resolutions from few seconds to minutes.
